Components

Hardware components used in the Smart Helmet for Rider Safety.

Hardware Overview

The Smart Helmet for Rider Safety consists of two Arduino-based modules communicating over Bluetooth. The helmet module is responsible for monitoring the rider, while the bike module controls the ignition system and receives data from the helmet.

Components Used

Component Quantity Purpose
Arduino UNO R3 2 Main controller for the helmet and bike modules.
HC-05 Bluetooth Module 2 Wireless communication between helmet and bike using a Master-Slave configuration.
MQ3 Alcohol Sensor 1 Detects the presence of alcohol in the rider's breath.
Eyelid Sensor 1 Monitors the rider for signs of drowsiness.
MPU6050 Accelerometer & Gyroscope 1 Monitors acceleration and tilt for crash detection.
12V Relay Module 1 Controls the motorcycle ignition circuit.
Buzzer 2 Provides an audible alert during emergency situations.
LED Indicators 2 Display the current operational status of the helmet and bike modules.
Rechargeable Battery Pack 1 Powers the helmet-side electronics.
