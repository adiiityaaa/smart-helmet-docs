Future Updates

Planned improvements and future development roadmap for the Smart Helmet for Rider Safety.

Development Roadmap

The current prototype demonstrates the core functionality of the Smart Helmet for Rider Safety. The modular architecture allows additional hardware and software features to be integrated in future versions without major changes to the existing system.

Safety Features

Feature Description
Portable Bike Module Convert the bike-side electronics into a detachable module that can be easily transferred between motorcycles.
Helmet Strap Detection Ensure the rider has securely fastened the helmet strap before allowing the motorcycle to start.
GPS Location Tracking Determine the rider's location after a crash for faster emergency response.
GSM Emergency Alerts Automatically send SMS alerts containing the rider's GPS coordinates to predefined emergency contacts.
Emergency SOS Button Allow the rider to manually request emergency assistance without crash detection.
Speed Monitoring Monitor vehicle speed continuously and warn the rider when the configured speed limit is exceeded.
Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) Continuously monitor tyre pressure and alert the rider when pressure falls below the recommended limit.
Rain Detection Detect rainfall and notify the rider of potentially slippery road conditions.
Air Quality Monitoring Monitor surrounding air quality and warn the rider when harmful pollutants exceed safe levels.

Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS)

Future versions of the Smart Helmet may incorporate Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS) features to further improve rider awareness and reduce the risk of accidents.

ADAS Feature Purpose
Blind Spot Detection Warn the rider when a vehicle is present in the blind spot.
Lane Departure Warning Alert the rider if the motorcycle unintentionally leaves its lane.
Forward Collision Warning Warn the rider when approaching another vehicle too quickly.
Rear Collision Warning Detect rapidly approaching vehicles from behind.
Geofencing Notify users when the motorcycle enters or exits predefined geographical areas.
Traffic Sign Recognition Identify important traffic signs such as speed limits and display warnings.
Adaptive Speed Warning Recommend safe riding speeds based on road and environmental conditions.
Safe Following Distance Warning Warn the rider when following another vehicle too closely.
Cornering Assistance Provide warnings during high-speed cornering and excessive lean angles.
Night Riding Assistance Improve rider awareness during low-light riding conditions.

Future Scope

The modular architecture of the Smart Helmet provides flexibility for future expansion. Upcoming versions may include mobile application integration, cloud-based data logging, ride analytics, over-the-air firmware updates, AI-assisted rider behaviour analysis, and machine learning algorithms for improved accident prediction and prevention.
