Recognition received for the Smart Helmet for Rider Safety project.

Competition Achievement

The Smart Helmet for Rider Safety project secured 3rd Position in the Mechatronics Project Exhibition Competition. The project was recognized for its practical implementation of embedded systems, wireless communication and rider safety technologies.

The competition evaluated projects based on innovation, technical implementation, practical applicability, presentation quality and overall system functionality.
