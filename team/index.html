Project Summary

The Smart Helmet for Rider Safety was conceived with the objective of enhancing motorcycle rider safety by integrating multiple embedded technologies into a single platform. The project combines alcohol detection, drowsiness monitoring, crash detection, Bluetooth-based communication and ignition control to improve rider awareness and reduce the risk of accidents.

The completed prototype was successfully demonstrated at the Mechatronics Project Exhibition Competition, where it secured 3rd Position.
